About

Our Carbon Footprint Calculator is a web-based tool designed to help individuals assess their environmental impact based on their daily lifestyle choices. By analyzing key factors such as transportation, electricity consumption, food habits, waste generation, and internet usage, this tool provides an estimate of your personal carbon footprint in terms of kilograms of CO₂ emissions per month.

We aim to raise awareness and promote sustainable living by empowering individuals to track and minimize their carbon footprint. By making small changes, we can collectively work towards a greener and more sustainable planet.
